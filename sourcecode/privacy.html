Privacy Policy

At Bloomy Flowers, we value the trust you place in us when you share your personal information with us. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled with care.

What Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you when you:

  • Place an order on our website
  • Create an account on our website
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or promotional emails
  • Contact us through our website or social media channels

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Your name and contact information (email address, phone number, and physical address)
  • Your payment information (credit card number, expiration date, and security code)
  • Your order history and preferences
  • Your IP address and browser type

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you about your orders, including order confirmations, shipping updates, and delivery notifications
  • Provide you with personalized recommendations and offers
  • Improve our website and services
  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented various measures to protect it, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information, such as payment details
  • Secure servers and databases to store your information
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems to prevent unauthorized access
  • Access controls and authentication procedures to ensure only authorized personnel can access your information
  • Regular security audits and testing to identify and address vulnerabilities

Who We Share Your Information With

We may share your personal information with:

  • Our payment gateway providers to process payments
  • Our shipping partners to fulfill orders
  • Our marketing partners to provide personalized offers and promotions
  • Law enforcement agencies or regulatory bodies, if required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Request a copy of your personal information in a portable format

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and tracking technologies to:

  • Improve your browsing experience on our website
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide targeted advertising and promotions

You can control the use of cookies and tracking technologies through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with changes in laws and regulations. We will notify you of any significant changes to this policy.

Effective Date
